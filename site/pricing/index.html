Common questions
What do I get for free?
Pick any one product and use it free with up to 1,000 requests per day. No credit card, no time limit. Download and run.
Can I buy just the products I need?
Yes. At $9.99/mo per product, you pick exactly what you need. If you end up using 3 or more, Pro is a better deal.
How does the license key work?
After purchase, you receive a license key via email. Set STOCKYARD_LICENSE_KEY=SY-... in your environment. The proxy validates it offline using cryptographic signatures — no phone-home, no network dependency.
What happens when my subscription expires?
Your proxy reverts to the free tier (1 product, 1,000 requests/day). No data loss, no config changes. Re-subscribe anytime.
What providers are supported?
OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Groq, Ollama, DeepSeek, xAI, Mistral, Cohere, Together, Fireworks, Perplexity, Replicate, Amazon Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, Google Vertex AI, and OpenRouter. Any OpenAI-compatible endpoint works out of the box.
Do I need to change my application code?
No. Point your OPENAI_BASE_URL at the Stockyard proxy. Your existing OpenAI SDK calls work unchanged.
Is there an enterprise plan?
For custom deployments, SLAs, volume licensing, or dedicated support, email enterprise@stockyard.dev.
