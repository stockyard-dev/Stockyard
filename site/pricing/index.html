Common questions
How does the license key work?
After purchase, you receive a license key via email. Set STOCKYARD_LICENSE_KEY=SY-... in your environment. The proxy validates it offline using cryptographic signatures — no phone-home, no network dependency.
What happens when my subscription expires?
Your proxy reverts to the free tier (1,000 requests/day). No data loss, no config changes. Re-subscribe anytime and your key activates instantly.
Can I switch between suite and individual products?
Yes. Suite keys unlock all 125 products. Individual keys unlock one product. You can upgrade to the suite at any time and your subscription is prorated.
What providers are supported?
OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Groq, Ollama, DeepSeek, xAI, Mistral, Cohere, Together, Fireworks, Perplexity, Replicate, Amazon Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, Google Vertex AI, and OpenRouter. Any provider with an OpenAI-compatible endpoint works out of the box.
Do I need to change my application code?
No. Point your OPENAI_BASE_URL at the Stockyard proxy. Your existing OpenAI SDK calls work unchanged. The proxy handles everything transparently.
Is there a free tier?
Yes. Every product includes a free tier with 1,000 requests per day. No credit card required. Download the binary and start using it immediately.
