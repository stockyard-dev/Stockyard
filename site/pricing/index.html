Frequently asked
Is Community really free?
Yes. All 6 apps, all 50+ modules, all 16 providers. No feature gating. The 10K request/mo limit and 3-user cap are the only restrictions. For most side projects and small teams, that's plenty.
What does Pro unlock?
Unlimited requests, users, retention, auto-backups, and email alerts. It's for teams running Stockyard on their own servers who've outgrown the free limits. You get a license key that removes the caps.
Why Cloud over Pro?
Zero ops. No server to maintain, no Docker, no deploys, no backup scripts. You get a managed endpoint in 30 seconds. Pro is for teams who want to self-host but need unlimited capacity.
Can I switch tiers?
Yes. Upgrade Community to Pro with a license key — same binary. Move to Cloud by exporting config via Exchange environment sync. Downgrade anytime.
Do you mark up LLM costs?
No. Stockyard never touches your API keys or charges per token. You pay your LLM providers directly. Stockyard pricing is for the platform only.
What counts as a request?
One LLM API call through the proxy = one request. Dashboard views, management API calls, and Exchange operations don't count.
