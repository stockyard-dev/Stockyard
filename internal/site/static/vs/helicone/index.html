When to choose Stockyard

You need more than observability — you want safety guardrails, cost controls, prompt management, workflows, and an audit trail in one tool. Or you need self-hosted deployment for data residency, compliance, or air-gapped environments.

When to choose Helicone

You specifically want a cloud-hosted observability layer with minimal setup. You’re not looking for a proxy or middleware — just logging, analytics, and prompt tracking as a managed service.

Observability vs. control

Helicone tells you what happened after a request. Stockyard sits in the request path and can act on it — blocking toxic content, enforcing cost caps, caching responses, falling back to another provider, or compressing prompts. Observability is one of Stockyard’s six apps, not the whole product.

Data ownership

With Helicone, your prompts and completions flow through their infrastructure. With Stockyard self-hosted, nothing leaves your network. For teams with data residency requirements, compliance mandates, or sensitive workloads, this is the deciding factor.
