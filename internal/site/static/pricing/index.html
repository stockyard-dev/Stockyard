Frequently asked
Is Community really free?
Yes. All 6 apps, all 70 modules, all 16 providers. No feature gating. The 10K request/mo limit and 3-user cap are the only restrictions. You'll need to handle your own server setup, SSL certificates, updates, and backups — but for hobbyists and tinkerers who want full control, it's a complete platform.
What does Pro unlock?
Unlimited requests, users, retention, auto-backups, and email alerts. It's for teams running Stockyard on their own infrastructure who've outgrown the free limits. You still manage your own servers and deployment — Pro removes the usage caps, not the ops work.
Why Cloud over self-hosted?
Self-hosting means you manage the server, keep the binary updated, configure SSL, set up backups, monitor uptime, and handle scaling as traffic grows. Cloud eliminates all of that. You get a managed endpoint in 30 seconds — we handle infrastructure, updates, backups, scaling, and monitoring. Most teams choose Cloud so they can focus on their product instead of their proxy.
Can I switch tiers?
Yes. Upgrade Community to Pro with a license key — same binary. Move to Cloud by exporting config via Exchange environment sync. Downgrade anytime.
Do you mark up LLM costs?
No. Stockyard never touches your API keys or charges per token. You pay your LLM providers directly. Stockyard pricing is for the platform only.
What counts as a request?
One LLM API call through the proxy = one request. Dashboard views, management API calls, and Exchange operations don't count.
