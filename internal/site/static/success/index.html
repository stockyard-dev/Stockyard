You're in.

Your license key is on its way to your inbox. Here's what to do next.

Step 1 — Check your email
Your license key will arrive in the next few minutes. It starts with SY- and looks like a long encoded string.
Step 2 — Set the environment variable
Add it to your shell profile for persistence:

export STOCKYARD_LICENSE_KEY=SY-your-key-here
Step 3 — Start the proxy
The proxy reads the key on startup and unlocks your tier automatically.

stockyard --config stockyard.yaml
Didn't get the email? Check your spam folder. If it's not there, look up your license at stockyard.dev/account using your email address. The key is also stored in your account and can be retrieved at any time.
